About Me

I am a public policy researcher trained at the Indian Institute of Technology, Tirupati, with academic grounding in economics and applied policy analysis. My interests lie in evaluating public programs, fiscal systems, and institutional design, particularly in contexts affecting vulnerable populations.

I have applied research experience at the Institute for Social and Economic Change (ISEC), Bengaluru, where I worked on large-scale climate advisory systems under the Varuna Mitra program. My role involved field research, stakeholder consultations, institutional diagnostics, and monitoring framework development.

Beyond academics, I have served as a Placement Representative for the MPP program at IIT Tirupati. I am a state-level yoga medalist and actively participate in debates and policy discussions.
