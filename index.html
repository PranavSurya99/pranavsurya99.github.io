About Me

I am a public policy researcher with academic training and applied experience in public finance, social protection, urban governance, and climate-resilient service delivery. My work focuses on using data, institutional analysis, and field-based insights to improve the design and implementation of public programs in developing economy contexts.

I am currently pursuing a Master’s in Public Policy at the Indian Institute of Technology Tirupati. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Economics (Honours) from Sri Sathya Sai Institute of Higher Learning, Prashanthi Nilayam, where I developed a strong foundation in economic theory, statistics, and applied policy analysis.

Beyond academics, I value discipline, teamwork, and sustained learning. I have represented at the state level in yoga and actively participate in policy discussions, debates, and development-focused reading.
