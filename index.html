About Me

I am a public policy researcher trained at the Indian Institute of Technology, Tirupati, with a strong academic foundation in economics, policy analysis, and program evaluation. My research interests focus on how public institutions design, finance, and implement policies that shape welfare outcomes, service delivery, and resilience among vulnerable populations.

I have applied research experience through my work with the Institute for Social and Economic Change (ISEC), Bengaluru, where I contributed to the design and evaluation of large-scale climate advisory services under the Varuna Mitra program, serving over two million farmers. My work involved field surveys, stakeholder consultations, institutional diagnostics, and the development of governance and monitoring frameworks.

Beyond academics, I have held leadership roles as a Placement Representative for the Master of Public Policy program at IIT Tirupati, coordinating employer engagement and career support activities. I actively participate in policy discussions, debates, and student initiatives. Outside academics, I practice yoga and have competed at the state level, which has strengthened my discipline and focus.
